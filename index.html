Introduction

Hosting is a service that allows companies and individuals to publish their website on the Internet. It is a place where the files and content of a website are stored, allowing them to be accessible from anywhere in the world via the web. There are several types of hosting available, including shared, dedicated, VPS and cloud hosting, each with its own features and pricing.

Hosting

What is web hosting?

What does it consist of?

According to the guide given by (IONOS, 2019), it is one of the basic technologies of the Internet, which is characterized by being a storage space for the publication of websites. In this respect, websites are similar to text documents, but unlike saving them only on your computer's hard drive, they must be stored on a powerful server that is connected 24 hours a day

Why is web hosting important?

Web hosting is a service that allows users to have their websites accessible on the Internet. Having a good hosting provider ensures that your website is available, and with the right resources, it works optimally at all times.

What are the characteristics of web hosting?

When talking about the characteristics of web hosting, it is important to take them into consideration, since this will allow us to make a "approximate" of the cost / benefit of web hosting, because the monthly cost of web hosting may vary, depending on factors as:

    Website size
  • Daily number of visits that the site receives
  • Site traffic at peak times
  • The accommodation characteristics that are required

So the features present in most web hosting are:
  • Disk space
  • Bandwidth and data transfer
  • E-mail accounts
  • FTP Access

How does it work?

How does web hosting work?

When you create an account with the web host, they allocate some space for the web site or web application on the server. You can access and manage this space from your hosting account panel. Similarly, you will gain access to other resources such as Random Access Memory (RAM), Central Processing Unit (CPU), and bandwidth. The percentage of server resources you get depends on the type of web hosting service you select.

What are the types of web hosting services?

  • dedicated hosting
  • cloud hosting

What are the requirements for modern web hosting?

    • SEO

    SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is an online marketing technique that optimizes a website to improve its visibility and ranking in the search results of search engines such as Google, Bing, etc.
    SEO is an ongoing process that involves both on-page (website content and structure) and off-page (inbound links) optimization to improve the relevance and authority of a website. On-page factors include the quality and relevance of content, website structure, title and description tagging, etc. Off-page factors include the number and quality of inbound links pointing to a website.
    It is important to keep in mind that SEO is an ongoing process and that search engine techniques and algorithms are constantly changing. Therefore, it is necessary to keep updated and adapt to changes in order to maintain a good performance in search results.

    SEM

    SEM (Search Engine Marketing) is a form of online marketing that includes both SEO and Search Engine Advertising (SEA). With SEM, companies can promote their products and services in search results by paying for ads and increasing their visibility in organic search results through SEO.
    SEM includes both organic search engine optimization (SEO) and buying ads in search results (SEA). With search engine advertising, companies can display their ads in search results when users search for relevant keywords. SEA is a fast and effective way to gain visibility and traffic to a website, but it requires an ongoing investment.


    Conclusions

    Web hosting is a service that allows users to host their websites on remote servers. This allows sites to be available online 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. There are many options available in the market, including shared hosting, dedicated hosting, cloud hosting, and more. When choosing a hosting provider, it's important to consider factors such as cost, availability, speed, security, and customer support. Also, it is important to choose a plan that meets the needs of your website, be it in terms of bandwidth, storage, and other specific features. In general, web hosting is a critical component to having a successful and well-functioning website.
